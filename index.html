About Maria Jackson

The hygienist leading the future of dental AI careers.

Maria spent more than 20 years in dentistry, 15+ as a practicing hygienist. But even with deep clinical expertise, she found herself unseen and undervalued by the tech industry.

So she did what most clinicians should never have to do: went back to school to earn a Master's Degree in Applied Digital Technology with a concentration in AI and Business Analytics — just to be taken seriously.

Once inside the AI world, she trained teams, mentored hygienists, spoke at industry events, and partnered with AI companies. And she realised something important: no dental professional should need another degree to transition into tech. They just need support, access, and someone who knows the way.

"What was once my struggle is now your advantage. That is why Dental AI Solutions was created."
