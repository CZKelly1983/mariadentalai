About Maria

I spent 15 years as a dental hygienist. I loved the work, but somewhere along the way, I started wondering: "Is there more for me?"

So I did what seemed necessary at the time: I went back to school and earned a Master's degree in Applied Digital Technology with a focus on AI and Business Analytics. It worked—companies took me seriously. But it shouldn't have had to be that way.

Now, after training 30+ dental offices, mentoring dozens of hygienists into tech roles, and partnering with leading AI companies, I realize: You shouldn't need another degree. You just need clarity, skills, and someone who understands your journey.

That's why I created Dental AI Solutions.
