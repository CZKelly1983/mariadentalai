About Maria

She's been exactly where you are.

I spent 15 years as a dental hygienist. I loved the work — but somewhere along the way, I started wondering if there was more for me.

So I did what seemed necessary: I went back to school and earned a Master's degree in Applied Digital Technology with a focus on AI and Business Analytics. It worked. But it shouldn't have had to be that way.

After training 30+ dental offices, mentoring dozens of hygienists into tech roles, and partnering with leading AI companies, I know this: you don't need another degree. You need clarity, skills, and someone who genuinely gets your journey.
