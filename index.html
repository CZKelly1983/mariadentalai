Why Maria built this

Maria spent more than 20 years in dentistry—15+ as a practicing hygienist. She had the skills. The expertise. The knowledge. But the tech industry didn't see her value.

So she did what most hygienists shouldn't have to do: she went back to school and earned a Master's Degree in Applied Digital Technology with a concentration in Business Analytics and AI.

Only then did companies take her seriously. But it shouldn't be that hard. Nobody should need another degree just to prove their worth.

Since breaking into tech, she's trained hygiene teams across 30+ offices, partnered with AI startups, and mentored hygienists into roles they never thought possible. And she realized something: You don't need another degree. You just need someone who knows the way.
