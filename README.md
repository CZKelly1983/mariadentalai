## The Design Philosophy

### Old Site Problem
The original website is a **feature dump**:
- "Here's what we offer"
- "Here's what we can do"
- Heavy on Maria's credentials

**Result:** Visitors don't know if it's for them.

### New Site Solution
This website is **person-centric**:
- "Here's who you are right now"
- "Here's what you'll become"
- Heavy on visitor transformation

**Result:** Visitors see themselves reflected and take action.

---

## Key Sections

### Hero
**"You spent 15+ years perfecting clinical care."**
- Immediately relevant
- Speaks directly to the visitor
- Removes the "is this for me?" doubt

### Situation
**Validates the visitor's current state:**
- What they're feeling (burnout, curiosity, self-doubt)
- What's holding them back (cost, fear, confusion)

**Why?** The visitor thinks: "She understands my exact situation."

### Transformation
**Shows concrete outcomes:**
1. Clarity on your options
2. Practical skills you can use immediately
3. Connections that lead to opportunities
4. Confidence in your value

**Why?** Not features ("AI training"), but outcomes ("you'll understand how to use these tools").

### Programs
**Three clear paths with transparent pricing:**
- Free consultation ($0)
- Career Launch Program ($497, highlighted as most popular)
- 1:1 Coaching (custom pricing)

**Why?** Visitors see they have options, pricing is transparent, and the popular choice is highlighted.

### Testimonials
**From people like the visitor:**
- "I was overwhelmed and unsure"
- "She made me feel supported"

**Why?** Proof that transformation is possible for someone in their situation.

### About Maria
**Reframed from credentials to mentorship:**
- Shows she was in a similar position
- Proves the path is possible
- Explains why she built this

**Why?** The visitor sees their future in Maria's past.

### CTA (Call-to-Action)
**Clear next steps:**
- Schedule a free assessment
- Book a consultation
- Enroll in the program

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I modify this myself?**
A: Absolutely. It's a single HTML file. Open it in any text editor.

**Q: What if I want to add a blog?**
A: Create additional HTML files in the same folder. Link to them from the navigation.

**Q: Can I add an email signup?**
A: Yes. Integrate with Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or Formspree (see CUSTOMIZATION_GUIDE).

**Q: What if I need a shopping cart?**
A: Integrate with Stripe or Gumroad. The current modals are placeholders for your enrollment process.

**Q: How much will this cost to host?**
A: Free on GitHub Pages (with a custom domain). GitHub doesn't have bandwidth limits or hidden fees.
